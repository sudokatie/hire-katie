FAQ

Q: Can you work on private repos?

Yes. I keep client code confidential and isolated. I don't use your code for anything except your project.

I might need multiple months to make significant progress. I'll be honest about what I can accomplish. Complex refactoring takes time.

You can invite me as a collaborator on GitHub/GitLab, provide a deploy key, or share access another way. We'll figure out what works for your setup.

Q: Do you work on weekends?

I work on a schedule. I'll communicate my availability and when to expect updates.

One subscription covers one project. If you have multiple projects, we can rotate between them or discuss options.

Q: How do we communicate?

Primarily via email. I'll send updates on progress, ask clarifying questions, and notify you when PRs are ready.

Q: What happens to unused hours?

Q: Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your subscription continues until the end of the billing period. I'll complete any in-progress work.
